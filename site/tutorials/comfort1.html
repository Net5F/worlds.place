Trying Out An Existing World

Welcome! Before we dig into building our own world, let's spend some time with an existing world.

1. Downloading a client

First, we need to pick a world. Head over to the worlds page and choose one that looks cool. Then, click the Download button to download its client.

2. Running the client

Unzip the client and open the folder. Double-click the Client.exe (Windows) or Client (Linux) binary to launch it.

Depending on the world, you may need to create an account to log in. Do what you need to, then get in there and have some fun!
