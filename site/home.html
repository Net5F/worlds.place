The Amalgam Engine

Easily create virtual worlds that support up to 2,000 players per server.

Fully open source, community driven.

Focused on creativity, innovation, and fun.

The Amalgam Engine is a C++/Lua-based engine for creating virtual worlds. Use it to prototype ideas, build small worlds for your friends, or even create full MMOs.

Why use The Amalgam Engine?

  • Faster than any other engine at building virtual worlds
    • Networking, physics, and many other necessary systems (e.g. items, inventory) are fully implemented out of the box
  • Higher player counts than any other engine
    • 1,000 - 2,000 players spread throughout the world (depending on world complexity and hardware)
    • Or, 200+ players in the same area (great for social worlds and event spaces)
  • Provides the tools needed to innovate
    • Prototype your virtual world ideas as easily as prototyping a single-player game in other engines
    • Build content collaboratively with a live, in-world "build mode"

Get Involved

For too long, virtual worlds have been controlled by giant, risk-averse corporations. By building tools that allow small teams to create virtual worlds, we hope to start a new golden age of innovation in the genre.

Whether you're a developer, artist, or just a fan of virtual worlds, we encourage you to get involved by joining the Discord.
